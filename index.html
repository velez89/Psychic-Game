About Me

Born and raised in Atlanta, I am first generation mainlander of two extraordinary Puerto Ricans. In stereotypical Latin fashion, I come from a large Catholic family of seven. I narrowly missed being the youngest of my 4 sisters by 11 minutes.

During the week, I work as an Underwriter for Interstate National Corp. On the weekends I transition to my other job as a SCUBA instructor. My underwriting gig pays the bills while the latter helps appease my wanderlust. So far may travels have taken me across Latin America, the Carribean, and Europe. I'm only bilingual in English and Spanish, but I have ambitions in advancing to polyglot status in the near future.
